

## Smart Handyman — 3-Page Professional Website

### Branding & Design System
- Colors: Red (#CC0000), Black (#111111), White (#FFFFFF)
- Bold, clean, professional feel with generous spacing
- User-uploaded logo used in navbar and footer

### Global Components
- **Sticky Navbar**: Black background, white text, logo left, nav links (Home, Services, Contact) right, hamburger menu on mobile
- **Footer**: Black background, logo left, business name + tagline center, phone/email right, copyright line

### Page 1 — Home (`/`)
- **Hero Section**: Split layout — left side with business name, tagline, location, body text, and red "Get a Free Quote" CTA; right side with contact form (Full Name, Contact Number, City, Service Needed dropdown, Additional Info textarea, red submit button). Dark background, white text. Form sends via EmailJS.
- **Trust Badges Strip**: 4 badges in a row — "Licensed & Insured", "Faithfully Serving Texas", "Quality Guaranteed", "No Job Too Small"
- **Services Preview**: "What We Do" heading, 6 service cards in grid with icons and descriptions, hover effects, links to Services page
- **Gallery Teaser**: "A Look at Our Work" heading, 6 placeholder project photos in grid, red "See All Our Work" button linking to Services page

### Page 2 — Services (`/services`)
- **Page Header**: Dark banner with heading and subheading
- **6 Service Sections**: Each with heading, description paragraph, and photo gallery. Alternating left/right layout for visual variety. Placeholder images with descriptive alt text for: Garage Door Services, Media Wall Designs, Kitchen Renovation, Accent Walls, Flooring & Carpet Removal, In-House Painting
- **Bottom CTA Banner**: "Ready to get started?" with red "Contact Us" button

### Page 3 — Contact (`/contact`)
- **Page Header**: Dark banner with heading and subheading
- **Two-column layout**: Left side with phone, email, location info; right side with full contact form connected via EmailJS

### Technical Details
- EmailJS integration for both forms (user will need to provide service ID, template ID, and public key)
- SEO meta titles and descriptions per page
- Fully mobile responsive
- Clean URLs: `/`, `/services`, `/contact`
- Placeholder images used until real photos are uploaded
